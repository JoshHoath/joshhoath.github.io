PAACET Successess

PAACET works under the guide of the Sustainable Development Goals set by the UN. Most of our work is under Goal #5: Gender Equaility. PAACET works with other orginaztions around the world in order to achieve gender equaility and empower all women and girls.

PAACET has worked tirelessly through out different areas of the world to work towards out goals. With different projects becoming success stories along the way.
