Our Mission

Solving human rights, post-conflict and structural violence in governments, businesses and not-for-profit organizations using ethically sound traditional conflict transformation techniques applied to training and research methodologies.

Our Story

Peace Africa Alliance Consulting, Educating and Training (PAACET) Centre is a private non-governmental and non-profit organization that was conceptualised at the European University Center for Peace and Conflict Studies (EPU) in Stadtschlaining, Austria in 2007. PAACET was fully established with legal status in 2015 in Alberta, Canada, where its headquarters is located. PAACET has its Africa Regional Office in Freetown, Sierra Leone. PAACET offers consultancies both in Canada and abroad, mainly through partnering with existing organizations and other consultants in Sierra Leone, presenting trainings in traditional methods of conflict resolution, human rights training, healing & reconciliation, and other related areas. Our work includes trainings at peace universities in Africa and Liberia, so far. Within five years, we will expand to other African and Middle East countries as opportunities

Our Identity

PAACET is an independent not-for-profit international peace and human rights organization. We share common values and interests with likeminded organizations, institutions, corporations and associations to promote global unity, peace, human rights and justice to ensure a life of dignity and respect for all humanity regardless of sex, race, religion or ethnic belonging.
